One of the most famous formulas in algebra is the solution to the quadratic equation. For an equation of the form:

a x 2 + b x + c = 0

The solutions for x are given by:

x = − b ± b 2 − 4 a c 2 a

This is known as the quadratic formula. The symbol ± tells us there are generally two solutions.
